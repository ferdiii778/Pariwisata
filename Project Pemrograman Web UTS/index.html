What About Bromo?

Mount Bromo is one of the volcanoes that is still active in Indonesia. This mountain, which has a height of 2,392 meters above sea level, is a mainstay destination in East Java. Mount Bromo stands proudly surrounded by a caldera or sea of sand covering an area of 10 square kilometers.

What About Yogyakarta?

Yogyakarta means Yogya which is hard, Yogya which is prosperous, while Ngayogyakarta Hadiningrat means Yogya which is prosperous and the most important. Another source says that the name Yogyakarta is taken from the name (mother) of the Sanskrit city of Ayodhya in the epic Ramayana.
